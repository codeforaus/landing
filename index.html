ABOUT


Code for Australia is an initiative that brings together designers, developers and entrepreneurs to build stuff that matters. Our aim is to make our cities more interactive, encourage more responsive government and create meaningful change through technology

PROBLEM

We interact with government and the broader community to discover the most pressing public issues.

IDEA

We believe design isn't just visual. Design thinking influenes how we think and respond to the issue at hand.

SOLUTION

We go to great lengths to create solutions that work in the real world. We prioritise goodwill and impact.
